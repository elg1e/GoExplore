GoExplore Guide!

GoExplore is designed to help you find out restaurants, accommodation, bars, and cafes in your chosen destination!
Here is a step by step guide to help you use the site!

Step 1
  • Press the search box and find your chosen city and press search.
Step 2
  • When GoExplore has found your city, press one of the buttons (Restaurants, accommodation, bars, cafes) and the map will give you the information you need.
Step 3
  • If you need to search for another city, press the reset button and then repeat the steps.
